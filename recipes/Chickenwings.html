Chicken Wings

Honey barbeque chicken wings

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 lbs. Chicken wings
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Paprika
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Egg

STEPS

  1. Thaw chicken properly.
  2. Rub with salt, paprika, and garlic
  3. Cover lightly with flour, dip in egg, and cover again with flour
  4. Line a baking tray with parchement paper and place chicken on tray
  5. Lightly oil the tray
  6. Pre-heat oven to 415 Fahrenheit
  7. Bake for 45 minutes, flipping halfway through
  8. Broil for 8 minutes
  9. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce
